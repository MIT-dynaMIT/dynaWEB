Taste of DynaMIT

We visit classrooms, parent nights, and science fairs in the Boston and Cambridge area, bringing with us fun activities as well as pamphlets and flyers for our free science program. These outreach sessions are filled with exciting, hands-on science experiments that we do right in the classroom to give a taste of what our program is like. If you are interested in having dynaMIT come to your school or have any questions, comments, or suggestions, please contact us at dynamit_board@mit.edu. We look forward to hearing from you!
